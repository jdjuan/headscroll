Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

In plain English, I took about 5000 pictures and managed to teach a machine what a tilted head was, and what it was not. In other words, I created a model using Google's Teachable Machine, put it on an Angular application, and scrolled a website every time the model told me there was a tilted head.


What's on the roadmap?

The next step is release HeadScroll as a native app (the kind you install on your computer) so that you can scroll any website without restrictions and with a better user experience. But just like I told my mom when I was born, keep your expectations down.
