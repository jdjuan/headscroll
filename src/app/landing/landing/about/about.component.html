About

We are Juan and Felipe, two Colombian software engineers based in Austria.

After playing guitar for about 20 years, Juan noticed he always interrupted his "delightful" concerts to scroll his tabs. A procrastination whim lured him into finding a solution. There wasn't any, yet.
Juan procrastinated 200 hours more trying to build HeadScroll just to realize he couldn't do it alone. He reached out to Felipe and convinced him they could finally become popular with this "one" idea. Felipe believed him. And here we are.
